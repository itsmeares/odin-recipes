Turkish Manti Recipe

Back to Homepage

Turkish Manti is a type of dumpling filled with spiced meat

Manti is a typically filled with lamb or ground beef, wrapped in thin dough and often served with garlic yogurt and a spiced oil or butter sauce. It is a traditional dish in Turkish cuisine, known for its small size and rich flavors. We will make a yogurt sauce and red pepper oil for our Manti in this recipe.

Ingredients

(This recipe yields 4 servings.)

Dough:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour, plus more for dusting
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • ½ teaspoon water, or as needed

Filling:

  • 2 onions, peeled
  • ½ pound ground beef
  • salt and pepper to taste

Red Pepper Oil:

  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon red pepper flakes

Yogurt Sauce:

  • 1 (8 ounce) container plain yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
